    About GS Agateko

    For over 5 years, we've been committed to providing exceptional education that prepares students for success in an ever-changing world.

    Our Mission

    Groupe Scolaire Agateko Mission is to promote integral education and transform Rwandan citizen able to resolve encountered problems and to contribute to their country sustainable development by creating teaching and learning environment offers opportunities for all learners, to promote literacy and numeracy and inspire young people a spirit of hard working with endurance in order to be agent of change.

    SCHOOL DIRECTION

    Groupe Scolaire Agateko is committed to providing a solid education that builds the capacity of Rwandan citizens to solve problems, face challenges, and contribute to the country's development. The school ensures equal learning opportunities for all students and emphasizes reading, writing, and numeracy skills. The students are also trained to uphold cultural values and work toward making positive changes in their communities.

    SCHOOL VALUES

    Groupe Scolaire Agateko realises the school mission achievement by holding some values such as: Punctuality, Cleanliness, Charity and Unity.

    SCHOOL VISION

    Groupe Scolaire Agateko aims to improve the quality of teaching and learning at both primary and secondary levels. The vision is that all students, both boys and girls, graduate with strong intellectual abilities, positive personal attitudes, and spiritual values that help them understand their nation and become active, competitive citizens. .

    CORE PRINCIPLES

    The school is dedicated to living out its mission through strong principles, such as: Respecting time Practicing good hygiene Showing love and unity Living these values daily in school and in the wider community
